About Me

Hi, My name is Tomas Matusek

Born in 1987 in the Czech Republic.
I was an active child with a love for sports such as cycling, football, and mountain climbing. Alongside these activities, I developed a strong curiosity for computers. I had many friends and cherished my youthful experiences. As I grew older, I felt the need for change and personal growth. This led me to move to Austria, where new opportunities rekindled my childhood passion for learning. In Austria, I found a new home, job, and friends, and I immersed myself in learning the German language, eventually achieving a B1 level through WIFI. During this time, I also had the opportunity to study web development at the Coding Academy am Wörthersee. My interest in the IT sector led me to self-study coding, where I encountered and overcame various challenges. Learning to program a website brought me immense satisfaction and a sense of accomplishment. I often spent long hours after work honing my skills, drawn to the creativity and problem-solving aspects of programming. This journey taught me not only the technical aspects of coding but also the importance of discipline, focus, and self-dedication. As I continue to develop my skills and knowledge, I am committed to pursuing a career in web development, both professionally and personally.
