About Me

Hi, My name is Tomas Matusek

Born in 1987 in the Czech Republic,
I was an active child who enjoyed various sports, such as cycling, football, and mountain climbing. I had many friends and cherished my youthful experiences. As I grew older, I sought new opportunities and challenges. This led me to move to Austria, where I found a new home, job, and friends. I also began learning German, which proved to be an essential skill for daily interactions and personal growth. The journey wasn't easy, but I progressed from A1 to A2 and eventually to B1 in my German language proficiency. It became clear that mastering the language was just one of many steps in achieving my goals and dreams. One of those dreams was to become a Web Developer. My interest in the IT sector led me to self-study coding, where I faced various challenges. Learning how to program a website brought me immense satisfaction and a sense of accomplishment. I often spent long hours after work honing my skills, drawn to the creativity and problem-solving aspects of programming. This journey taught me not only the technical aspects of coding but also the importance of discipline, concentration, and self-dedication. As I continue to develop my skills and knowledge, I am committed to pursuing a career in web development, both professionally and personally.
